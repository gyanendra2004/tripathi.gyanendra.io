Dr. Gyanendra Nath Tripathi, PhD (Engg.), a researcher and educator working on embedded systems and intelligent robotics, with applications to humanoid and rehabilitation robots. He graduated Bachelor ofEngineering in Electronic and Communication from Kumaun University (Birla Institute of Applied Sciences) 2004-2008, and then completed Master of Engineering in Electronic Product Design and Technology (Embedded for robotics and Artificial Neural Network) 2008-2010, After masters he worked for 2.5 year as Assistant Professor 2010-2013 (LPU,ITM Gurgaon, MITS Laxmangarh) . He completed his PhD in Brain Inspired Robotics and worked on Humanoid Robot Motion Planning 2013-2016. After PhD he worked with top Japanese multinationals (Renesas, Hitachi, ADVICS, Panasonic) in Embedded Design for control related to Factory Automation and Robotics for 8+ years, currently working as Researcher at Tsukuba University while looking for an academic position, Japan.
